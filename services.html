Power Automate Solutions

New Starter Workflows

We design end-to-end automated provisioning flows. From the moment HR confirms a new hire, our systems automatically create accounts in Microsoft 365, assign appropriate security groups, and trigger personalized welcome emails.

Leaver Process Automation

Securely offboard staff with one-touch automation. Our flows disable 365 accounts, revoke access permissions, and re-route mailboxes in seconds, ensuring your digital perimeter remains secure at all times.

Intelligent Approval Processes

Remove bottlenecks with dynamic approval routing. Whether it's purchase requests or project sign-offs, we build automated approval paths that notify managers instantly, allowing for quick decisions directly from email or Teams.
